How is WHACKD's Circulating Supply calculated?

Simple. Max Supply (1,000,000,000 WHACKD) - Burnt tokens = Circulating Supply.

Due to WHACKD's deflationary mechanism, the circulating supply will go lower as time passes and usage increases.
